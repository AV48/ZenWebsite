Box Breathing Simulation

About Box Breathing

The box breathing technique is a simple breathing exercise that is meant to return breathing to its normal rhythm after a stressful experience. To complete, a person inhales for four seconds, holds the air for four seconds seconds, exhales for four seconds, and then holds the air for four seconds. Click the button to start.
