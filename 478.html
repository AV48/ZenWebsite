About 4-7-8 Breathing

The 4-7-8 breathing technique is a simple breathing exercise that has been proven to decrease heart rate and blood pressure, helping people fall asleep. It is also an easy exercise for someone to ease their anxiety. To complete, a person inhales for four seconds, holds the air for seven seconds, and then exhales for eight seconds.
