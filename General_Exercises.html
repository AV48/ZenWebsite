General Exercises

5-4-3-2-1 Method

How To: First, start off by taking some few deep breaths. Then, identify 5 things you can see around you, notice 4 things you can touch, listen to 3 things you can hear, smell 2 things in your environment, and taste 1 thing (if possible).

Benefits: Reduces Stress and Anxiety, Improves Breathing, Increases Mindfulness, and more

Nature Walk

How To: Go for a walk outside. You can do this directly outside your house, or find a trail or park. As you walk, take note of your surroundings, or play music or an audiobook. Nature walks provide an opportunity for physical activity and improved mental health.

Benefits: Stress Reduction, improved mood, enhanced cognitive function, and boosted immunity.

EFT

How To: Start by tapping lightly with your fingers on the top of your head, then slowly tap between you eyebrows, then the sides of you eyes, move to you chin then eventually your collar bone. Repeat as needed.

Benefits: Releases emotional blocks, reduces stress and anxiety, improves focus, and calms the nervous system.
